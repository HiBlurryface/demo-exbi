What is exbi?

Exbi is a Cryptocurrency payment gateway.

Most online stores accept Visa and MasterCard, and more advanced ones accept PayPal. We offer a solution allowing you to receive crypto as payment for your goods or services. Withdraw your earnings to any crypto wallet of your preference.

How it works

01Create account with exbi

Pick your preferred cryptos to accept, and respond to the basic questionary to tell us more about your business and yourself.

02Install EXBI to your E-shop

Our widget is simple and blends into your online store design. Customers will follow intuitive steps to make a payment.

03Get crypto and analyse payments

Receive your first crypto transaction on your first day. Our instinctive admin panel will give you valuable business insights.
